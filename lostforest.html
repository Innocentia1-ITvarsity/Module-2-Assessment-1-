LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest isn’t hard to find — it’s just hard to see. Tucked in among the trees near the mokey trail, Treeline Trial Challenge Course, and Rugged Ascent Climbing Wall. In addition, the Lost Forest operates as a home base for fishing, hiking, and disc golf.
